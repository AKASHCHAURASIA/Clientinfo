ThemeQuarry, Our New Marketplace is Live!

Oct 9, 2016 Aigars Silkalns General

TL;DR Visit ThemeQuarry to submit your theme for sale today and get 70% commission.

Introduction

We have been working hard on a new marketplace for themes. We realized that with over 200k visitors per month, our website is ready to take on some new business. So, we decided to allow developers and designers to sell their own templates on our website.

ThemeQuarry is now live and ready to receive themes for sale. It provides a variety of features for seller and buyers. Let's discuss the most prominent features here.

Sellers Get 70%

Our commission policy is straightforward. Exclusive items get 70% of each sale while non-exclusive ones get 55%. The seller can change exclusivity any time they wish. We believe that you are the owner of the theme and are entitled to the full profits.

Seller's Dashboard & Statistics

Statistics Page

Theme Performance Page

The marketplace gives the sellers a powerful statistics dashboard. Sellers get information about sales, views and conversion rate of each theme as well as revenue statistics such as daily and monthly profit. They are also supplemented with visual charts to gain a better understanding of their sales behavior.

Quick Reviews

We hate the wait just as much as you do! Other marketplaces may take up to a week to either accept or reject your submission. We promise you, we won't take more 24 hours. In order for us to accept your theme, we have to make sure it does not violate any of our submission guideline. Also, unlike other marketplaces, if you theme is not accepted the first time, you can submit as many times as you'd like as long as you attempt to make the necessary improvements.

Keep In Touch With Your Customers

Currently, buyers can contact the sellers directly through comments and emails. But, we are working on a feature that will allow authors to notify customers of updates, issues and more. We believe that this feature gives sellers the power of a better customer service. Who likes bad customer service?

The Buyer's Experience

User Account Page

Order Invoice

Buyers are entitled to get only the best products and customer service and that's what we hope to achieve. Therefore, we made sure that our payment system is fully secure using PayPal as our payment gateway provider. Also, all buyers receive free product updates and can download them at anytime from their account page. Buyers are provided with all of the information they need for a successful shopping experience.

How Sellers Get Paid

PayPal Features

PayPal Mobile App

Our payment schedule is simple. On the 15th of every month, we transfer all the profits a seller has made during the past month to their PayPal account. There is no minimum threshold on the profits. This guarantees that we have received all payments from our buyers and that no refunds were issued during that month.

To get paid, you need an account with PayPal. This allows us to securely send payments to your account. Also, payments from PayPal to PayPal accounts are immediate, so there is no further waiting on your side.

Convinced?

Submit your template today at ThemeQuarry and start making money! We promise, you won't regret it.

Questions and Comments

If you have question about ThemeQuarry, you can send us an email via our contact from and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
